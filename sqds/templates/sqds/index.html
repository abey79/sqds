How to use

Where do I find stuff here?

Start by navigating to one of our guilds with the buttons above. This will bring you to the guild page, with a list of players and related stats. You can click on player names to go to the corresponding page, or inspect all of the guild's unit by clicking on the related link. You got the idea -- if its blue, click on it and see where it brings you.

How can I register who I am?

Navigate to your page (e.g. go to PREPARE's page, then click on your name in the list) and click on the "I am player name" button. Once done, the top toolbar will offer direct access to your and your guild's pages.

How can I compare myself with an opponent?

Navigate to your page (if you registered a profile, you can access your page from the top toolbar), enter the opponent's ally code in the "Compare with" text field, and click the "Compare" button. The formatting is irrelevant: you may leave the dashes or not, you may surround the ally code with text such as commas, you could even paste a swgoh.gg profile URL and it would work.

How can I compare myself with the rest of my GA pool?

Navigate to your page and enter all of your opponents' ally codes in the GA pool text field. Again, the formatting is irrelevant. Click on the "Analyse" button. The page will take a few minutes to load while the opponents' data is downloaded. From the GA pool comparison page, you can compare with individual players, see their units, etc. by clicking on the corresponding links. Note: once a GA pool is loaded, you can visit it again without entering the ally codes again. A list of the past 10 GA pools is shown on your player page.

About this site

What is this site?

This site exists because I (hhip) wanted to learn about things like web apps, Python, Django, DevOps, etc. and figured it would be a fun way to go about it.

Okay but what is this site to me?

At its core, it provides an efficient way to list, inspect and compare all of the guild's stats, both for player and toons. I will also be adding various features such as player vs. player comparison (Grand Arena) or guild vs. guild comparison (Territory War).

Who is this site for?

Me (see first question above). Also, members of PREPARE and PREPAIRED guilds.

Aren't you basically duplicating swgoh.gg and/or Discord bots?

See first question above. Also, you will find some unique metrics/stats/lists that are impossible or difficult to obtain from other sources.

How fresh is the data?

PREPARE and PREPAIRED data is updated every 4 hours. Data from other guilds/players is loaded on demand, and then not kept up-to-date. Date and time of last update is usually displayed somewhere.
