Introduction

What is this site?

This site exists because I (hhip) wanted to learn about things like web apps, Python, Django, DevOps, etc. and figured it would be a fun way to go about it.

Okay but what is this site to me?

At its core, it provides an efficient way to list, inspect and compare all of the guild's stats, both for player and toons. I will also be adding various features such as player vs. player comparison (Grand Arena) or guild vs. guild comparison (Territory War).

Who is this site for?

Me (see first question above). Also, members of PREPARE and PREPAIRED guilds.

Aren't you basically duplicating swgoh.gg and/or Discord bots?

See first question above. Also, you will find some unique metrics/stats/lists that are impossible or difficult to obtain from other sources.

How fresh is the data?

PREPARE and PREPAIRED data is updated every 4 hours. Data from other guilds/players is loaded on demand, and then not kept up-to-date. Date and time of last update is usually displayed somewhere.

Possibly. Let me know about it.
